01 — Profile

Brands and agencies hire me when the work has to land.

I’m Kenneth Robin, a creative director and motion designer in Los Angeles. For over a decade I’ve helped brands — and the agencies behind them — turn strategy into motion: brand films, broadcast identities, title sequences and launch campaigns that earn attention and keep it.

I direct and design, from first concept to final frame, so the idea that wins the room is the idea that ships — with nothing lost in the handoff.
